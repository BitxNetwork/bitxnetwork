How it Works

The Bit-X is a Volume Based Dividends Platform which pays BITX token holders 10% dividends through buying/selling volume. Holders receive their ETH divs proportionally and instantly, relative to the total BITX circulating supply.

  • Read More

1-2

1. Get some ETH - Send to a Web 3.0 wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet.
2. Purchase BITX, You will receive BITX for ETH and pay the 10% div fee to all token holders

3-4

3. Earn Dividends - Any new buy or sell will trigger you to receive dividend in your earnings.
4. Reinvest ETH dividends for more BITX tokens and increase your overall percentage of the supply.

5-6

5. Withdraw Dividends - Withdraw ETH directly to your wallet, the Smart Contract does the hard part.
6. Earn Consistent ETH, all dividends are paid in Ethereum only. Simple and transparent.
